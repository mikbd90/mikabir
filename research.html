Research Focus Areas

01
Cancer Therapeutics & Tumor Modeling
Orthotopic TNBC, AOM/DSS colorectal, and MNU-induced breast cancer models to study drug efficacy and MOA.
02
Obesity & Metabolic Disorders
Diet-induced obesity models studying the intersection of metabolic dysfunction and cancer progression.
03
Infectious Disease & HTP Screening
Anti-bacterial synergy, bacterial resistance, and therapeutic regimen development using high-throughput platforms.
04
Tumor Microenvironment (TME)
Immune cell infiltration, cytokine profiling, and spatial biomarker discovery under pharmacological intervention.
05
PK/PD & Translational Pharmacology
Target engagement and pharmacodynamic readouts integrated with tissue-level findings for early drug discovery.
06
Natural Products & Thrombolysis
Plant-derived metabolites, antioxidant activity, thrombolytic potential, and anticancer properties from Asian medicinal plants.
